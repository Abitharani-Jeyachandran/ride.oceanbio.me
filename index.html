    About Us

    The Ocean Ride project is a 13 days bicycle ride around the coastal districts of Sri Lanka, covering over 1300+km to promote marine conservation, create awareness among the local population about the importance of taking care of the ocean, responsible utilization of ocean resources, and creating inspiration to impact the future generation.

    Who are we

    We are young and passionate advocates for marine conservation, part of the Ocean Biome social enterprise, voluntarily taking on the challenge of riding over 1300km around the coastal districts of Sri Lanka on bicycles to spread awareness and be an inspiration.


    What is our mission

    To inspire the young generation to proactively work towards protecting the ocean and its resources around Sri Lanka by providing motivation, raising awareness and conducting education.

    Timeline

    We will be covering the entire coastline of the island, crossing over 10 districts, 6 provinces, 140+ towns & villages with 11 major waypoints in coastal towns where will be conducting short but inspiring awareness sessions at.
